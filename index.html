About Atomica

It all begins when I was sitting in class thinking "how could I use my coding skills to make chemistry simpler for myself?". Using C++ I simplified the entire PTable and got answers easier for every questions for my homework, this inspired me to do the same for every one of my subjects. One day I showed the software to one of my friend and they liked it, and wanted me to simplify each of their subject too. Then with my 2 brain-cells of HTML knowledge I tried to convert the C++ code to HTML and now here it is, Atomica.
